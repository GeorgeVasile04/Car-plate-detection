The license plate datasets use two coordinate formats to define bounding boxes:

XML Format (DataV2 & DataV3)

Uses (xmin, ymin, xmax, ymax) coordinates
Defines the top-left and bottom-right corners of the box
TXT Format (Data folder)

Uses (x, y, w, h) format
Where (x,y) is the top-left corner and w,h are dimensions
In both systems:

Origin (0,0) is at the image's top-left corner
X increases rightward, Y increases downward
All coordinates are in pixels
The boxes represent rectangular regions surrounding license plates
When visualizing boxes from XML format, they're drawn using: plt.Rectangle((xmin, ymin), width=xmax-xmin, height=ymax-ymin)
